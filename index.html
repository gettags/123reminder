Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to 123Reminder ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name (for personalization)
  • Email address (for authentication and notifications)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Reminder content (text you enter in the calendar)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our reminder service
  • Send you email notifications (if enabled)
  • Personalize your experience
  • Improve our service
  • Communicate with you about updates or issues

4. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored: All data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

Data Access: We (123Reminder administrators) have technical access to the database for maintenance and support purposes. However, we will never read your personal reminder content unless:

  • You explicitly request technical support requiring data access
  • Required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of 123Reminder, our users, or the public

5. Your Rights (India - DPDP Act 2023 Compliance)

Under Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your reminder data
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of email notifications anytime

6. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service providers (Supabase for hosting, Resend for email delivery) - under strict data protection agreements
  • When required by law

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We use Google AdSense for advertisements, which may use cookies for ad personalization.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, data requests, or questions: Click here to contact us

As per Indian law, our Grievance Officer can be contacted at:

Contact Grievance Officer
Response Time: Within 24 hours
